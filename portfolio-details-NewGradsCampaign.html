The Challenge

How to start with the right foot in a relationship with your online audience after entering your database? In a world where everything is digital, personalization is a must. Starting a trustworthy relationship is game-changing to keep an individual engaged in a long-term relationship. One of the George Brown Alumni Team's primary goals is to create a life-long relationship based on trust and recognition. In 2020, a new challenge took place. After COVID-19 hit, the convocation had to be postponed, and the team needed a new creative way to connect to these 5k new graduates entering our database.

The Journey

Showing genuine care about your audience and listening to them is the best way to increase engagement in a lifetime of communication. In the Education Industry, it is no different. We decided to create a personalized video and email campaign, including the graduate name and each Deanâ€™s message on it. Also, the alumni team hired a vendor to pull together a video and its personalization. We created this campaign for web and email channels.

Seven videos, seven emails, and seven landing pages were created for each centre to maximize the engagement.

My Role

I played the facilitator and executer role for this campaign. As the primary contact with the vendor and the web team to develop the video personalization, I also created all the personalized emails using HTML, CSS, and JS coding. The emails were created using the Salesforce Marketing Cloud platform. I was responsible for testing and made sure the personalization was working correctly.

The Outcome

The campaign goal was to achieve 22% OR and 8% of CTOR. This campaign achieved 31% OR and 87% CTOR, which is 140% the OR target and 218% of its CTOR target.

Why is this relevant

This campaign proved the power of a personalized campaign, and it achieved the highest Click-To-Open Rate in the last 5 years.
