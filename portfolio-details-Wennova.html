The Challenge

The global COVID-19 pandemic has forced businesses to prioritize digital transformation in order to survive, remain relevant and thrive. However, many small businesses do not have the knowledge or the budget to search for help. Over 3 million Ontarians are employed by small businesses, showing how vital they are for the local economy. Looking for ways to support our local community during these challenging times, we have created Wennova.

The Journey

Wennova is a volunteer project to support small businesses to become digital, not only from the marketing side but also from the entire strategy. It connects the small businesses to a network that can help them with their needs at no cost. The main idea is to provide guidance and teach small businesses how to create and survive in the digital world. In order to capture some leads to start the project, a website and LinkedIn posts were created.

My Role

I was the idealizer of the project. After contacting some colleagues, we decided to move forward with the project and help the local and international businesses become digital.

The Outcome
