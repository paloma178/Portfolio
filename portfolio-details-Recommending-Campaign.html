Recommending Campaign

Home Projects

The Context

George Brown Alumni Relations reports on the number of recommendations collected per year. In Jan 2019, we were close to finish our Fiscal Year without any recommendations.

The Project

The Alumni Team decided to offer an $5 Tim Horton’s cards incentive to those 50 graduates who first submit a recommendation on the George Brown Alumni Facebook page.

The Goal

To get new recommendations on our Alumni Facebook page from the alumni audience, and to meet the 50 recommendations target by March, 2019

The Contest Actions

1. Leave the recommendation and ​ 2. Confirm their name, graduate information, and current address within a FB direct message.

Facebook Performance

The posts performed extremely well on Facebook with an engagement rate of 12.6%. Because it was successful early on, there was no need to go ahead with paid ads. Overall, there were 103 Facebook reviews left during the campaign month – 72 verified grads! ​

Twitter Performance

Engagement was low on Twitter for this campaign at 0.8% which is to be expected as it was mainly designed for Facebook. The Twitter posts were to support our audience being redirected to Facebook.

Emails Performance

An audience was created based on a simple survey shared in the monthly newsletter. This survey collected 125 users who were willing to submit a recommendation. When the campaign started, an awareness message was sent to this audience, following for an personalized email. The strategy converted to 41 submissions, 53% Open rate, and 42% Click to Open Rate.

Project Info

To get 50 new recommendations on our Alumni Facebook page.
